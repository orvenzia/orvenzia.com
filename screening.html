Free Screening (5 min)

Answer 13 buyer‑aligned questions. We’ll respond with your OCI score and next steps.

1. Does the company have an environmental and climate policy?
(Covers energy, CO₂, water and waste – documented and updated)
2. Are energy, water, fuel and gas consumption measured and recorded?
(kWh, m³, liters, tonnes – metered or collected from invoices)
3. Has the company set reduction targets for energy and CO₂?
(Time-bound, measurable, linked to operations)
4. Is waste managed and recycled?
(Selective sorting, % recycled, evidence packs)
5. Are materials and resources managed?
(EPDs for steel, concrete, asphalt; recycled content where feasible)
6. Is Scope 3 data from suppliers collected and reported?
(Transport, packaging, purchased materials)
7. Does the company manage occupational health and safety?
(OHS policy, TRIR/LTIFR, audits, certifications)
8. Are employee surveys or engagement assessments conducted?
(Satisfaction, retention, culture)
9. Are equality, diversity and inclusion addressed?
(Guidelines, recruitment, gender/age/ethnicity KPIs)
10. Does the company have a Supplier Code of Conduct (CoC)?
(Coverage %, documented acceptance, right-to-audit)
11. Is there a whistleblower system in place?
(Anonymous, accessible, staff and suppliers)
12. Does the company have an anti-corruption and ethics policy?
(Organisation-wide, subcontractors included)
13. Are ESG risks assessed at management/board level?
(Annual risk assessments, board oversight, documented outcomes)
