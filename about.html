About Orvenzia — ESG Consulting That Gets SMEs Buyer-Ready

We help SMEs pass buyer ESG checks with speed, precision, and audit-ready documentation.

Mission

Make SMEs pass buyer ESG checks with speed and precision, while improving environmental outcomes.

Vision

Be the trusted ESG partner for SMEs in Europe, ensuring no company loses contracts due to ESG gaps.

Who we are

Orvenzia is an Aalborg-based ESG consulting firm (CVR: 45828697) dedicated to preparing SMEs for enterprise buyer requirements across Europe.

  • Standards we align with: CSRD/ESRS, GHG Protocol (Scopes 1–3), ISO 14001/9001 alignment, SBTi readiness.
  • Deliverables: policy baseline & documentation, buyer-facing ESG advisory report, supplier scope-3 data support, evidence & wording.

How we work

  1. Step 1: Screening (Free): 11 focused questions → materiality snapshot.
  2. Step 2: Advisory Report: gaps, risks, exact wording buyers expect, evidence list.
  3. Step 3: Implementation: policies, process flows, supplier engagement & KPI setup.
  4. Step 4: Continuous Monitoring: optional support to maintain buyer compliance.

Why choose us

  • Buyer-first. We write in the language procurement expects—fast approvals, fewer back-and-forths.
  • Evidence-ready. Every claim links to policies, data, or process proof.
  • Scope-3 practical. Supplier templates & flows that SMEs can actually run.
  • Fixed pricing & clear timelines. No surprises—see our packages and SLAs.
  • 50+ SMEs guided since 2023. Proven experience with fast-track results.
  • 100% buyer acceptance. All reports delivered have passed buyer checks.
